Our story

NetsaEDU was founded by Robel Alemayehu, a second-year Software Engineering student at Addis Ababa University. During his first year, Robel found it difficult to access reliable study guides and past exams. After talking to classmates and high school peers, he realized this was a shared problem across Ethiopia. This inspired him to build a centralized, free platform where students can find academic resources, upload their own, and help each other succeed. What began as a small idea has now become a growing student-powered learning movement.
